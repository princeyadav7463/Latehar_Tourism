Welcome to Latehar!

Discover the lush green landscapes, majestic waterfalls, rich wildlife, and historical sites of Latehar. Whether you are a nature lover, adventurer, or peace seeker, Latehar offers the perfect getaway into the heart of Jharkhand's beauty. Plan your visit and experience unforgettable memories.

Lodh Falls

Experience the thunderous beauty of Jharkhand's tallest waterfall surrounded by nature.

Netarhat

Watch breathtaking sunrises and sunsets from the Queen of Chotanagpur, nestled in pine forests.

Palamu Fort

Explore the rich history of Palamu with this hidden fort inside the Betla forest region.
