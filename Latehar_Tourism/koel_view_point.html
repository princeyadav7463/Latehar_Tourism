Koel View Point, Netarhat

Koel View Point in Netarhat, Jharkhand, is a picturesque and tranquil destination offering panoramic views of the scenic Koel River and the lush forests of the Chotanagpur Plateau. It's especially popular during sunrise and sunset hours, when the landscape glows with golden hues and misty valleys.

Significance

Although Koel View Point does not have an extensively recorded history, its importance is closely linked to Netarhat's past. During British colonial times, Netarhat was developed as a summer hill station because of its cool, refreshing climate and its resemblance to the European highlands. The serene beauty of Koel View Point was one of the reasons the British established retreats and schools in the region.

Today, Koel View Point stands as a favorite among nature lovers, photographers, and tourists looking to soak in the untouched beauty of the region. The tranquil ambiance, forested hills, and river valley make it an ideal spot for peace and relaxation.

Location

The viewpoint is located just about 3 km from the main Netarhat area and is easily accessible by road. It’s one of the must-visit spots when exploring Netarhat.
