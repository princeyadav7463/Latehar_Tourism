Pine Forest - Netarhat

Nestled in the scenic hills of Netarhat in Latehar district, the Pine Forest is a stunning destination known for its calm ambiance, lush greenery, and captivating landscapes. This natural treasure is ideal for those seeking peace, nature walks, and scenic photography.

Natural Beauty & Significance

The area features dense pine trees spread across rolling hills, offering a cool and serene escape from city life. The forest is not only beautiful but also holds cultural and ecological importance, with local legends and centuries-old forest preservation efforts.

Things to Do

  • Enjoy nature walks and trekking through the pine trails
  • Experience the morning mist and birdsong
  • Picnic with friends and family under shaded areas
  • Capture scenic shots of Netarhat valley from clearings

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Pine Forest is between October and March when the weather is cool and the forest is especially vibrant. Early mornings and evenings offer the most serene views and magical lighting for photography.

Nearby Attractions

  • Magnolia Sunset Point – 10 km
  • Upper and Lower Ghaghri Falls – 4-10 km
  • Netarhat Sunrise Point – Nearby
  • Betla National Park – Around 90 km

Whether you're a nature lover, photographer, or just someone looking for a peaceful getaway, Pine Forest in Netarhat promises a memorable experience.
